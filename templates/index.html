Regulate COVID-19 Through Our Application

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus which has wreak havoc and caused an ongoing worldwide pandemic. Social distancing is an effective measure against the novel CoronaVirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) pandemic.

Our application helps regulate the spread of COVID-19 by detecting pedestrians violating social distancing norms in a given video.

WORKFLOW

INPUT

- Video file in which you need to detect the social distancing violation

- Location at which the video is recorded

- Detector that has to be used: Select YOLO for fast results, Faster R-CNN for accurate results

PROCESS

- Detect pedestrians in each frame of the video

- Mark the pedestrians violating social distancing

- Calculate several statistics related to social density

OUTPUT

- Video file containing social distancing violations

- Several statistics that help you determine the status of social distancing in the video
